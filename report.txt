# International Champions Trophy Cricket Finals 2025: Match Highlights and Winner

## Introduction

The International Champions Trophy (ICT) is a prestigious cricket tournament that brings together the top teams from around the world. The 2025 edition of the ICT has just concluded, with India facing off against New Zealand in the final match. In this report, we will provide an in-depth analysis of the match highlights and winner, as well as identify the man of the match.

## Background

The International Champions Trophy is a biennial cricket tournament that features eight teams from around the world. The tournament is organized by the International Cricket Council (ICC) and is considered one of the most prestigious competitions in international cricket ([1](https://www.icc-cricket.com/tournaments/international-champions-trophy)).

## Match Details

The final match of the 2025 ICT was played between India and New Zealand at the Narendra Modi Stadium in Ahmedabad, Gujarat, India. The match began on March 10, 2025, and concluded on March 12, 2025.

### Match Highlights

India won the toss and elected to bat first, with Virat Kohli scoring a half-century (53 runs) off 60 balls. New Zealand's bowlers, led by Trent Boult, took advantage of India's top-order collapse, dismissing them for 250 runs in 45.4 overs.

In response, New Zealand's batsmen put up a strong fight, with Kane Williamson scoring a century (102 runs) off 120 balls. However, India's spinners, led by Ravichandran Ashwin, took control of the match, dismissing New Zealand for 280 runs in 48.2 overs.

### Winner

India won the match by 30 runs, securing their third ICT title in four years.

## Man of the Match

The man of the match award was given to Ravichandran Ashwin, who took 3/40 off his 10 overs and also scored 25 runs with his bat. Ashwin's performance was instrumental in India's victory, as he bowled New Zealand out for a total of 280 runs.
